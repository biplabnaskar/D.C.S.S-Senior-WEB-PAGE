Us

D.C.S.S (Dhakuria Club Samannay Samity) is a dynamic and passionate corporate cricket club based in the vibrant city of Kolkata, specifically located in Dhakuria. Over the past three years, this formidable club has showcased their exceptional cricketing skills in numerous tournaments, leaving an indelible mark on the local cricketing circuit. Known for their camaraderie and sportsmanship, D.C.S.S has carved a niche for itself as a formidable force on the field, while also fostering a close-knit community off it. Apart from their sporting prowess, D.C.S.S also takes pride in its commitment to community engagement, regularly organizing cricketing events in Dhakuria, aiming to spread the joy of the sport and make a positive impact on the lives of the less fortunate. In essence, D.C.S.S is more than just a cricket club; it is a symbol of passion, unity, and dedication to both the sport and the community it calls home. Their journey so far has been nothing short of inspiring, and there's no doubt that they will continue to shine as a beacon of excellence in the world of corporate cricket for years to.
